About SmartCT

SmartCT is an intelligent diagnostic system designed to assist healthcare professionals in identifying abdominal trauma using advanced CT scan analysis. Our goal is to enhance early detection and decision-making by leveraging modern computing and medical imaging technology.

Our Mission

The mission of SmartCT is to reduce diagnosis time, improve treatment outcomes, and support medical teams with reliable AI-driven tools for abdominal trauma cases.

How It Works

The system uses a combination of image processing, machine learning, and a clean web interface to analyze CT scans. It flags potential trauma regions and provides suggestions to assist radiologists in faster diagnosis.

Team & Development

SmartCT was developed by a team of passionate students and researchers committed to bridging the gap between healthcare and technology. The project continues to evolve with a focus on usability, accuracy, and real-world deployment in emergency care.
